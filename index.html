About Morgana

Morgana may look like an ordinary cat, but he is much more!
He is a member of the Phantom Thieves, who use their personas to change the hearts of evil people. Morgana is a cat of many skills within the Phantom Thieves, he shares navigation duties with Futaba Sakura and he also joins in with the fighting! He was created in the metaverse, but currently lives in the real world in the attic of Cafe Leblanc with his best friend, Ren Amamiya. Morgana spends his days napping, eating meat (especially tuna) and following Ren around, he even goes to school with Ren (but he stays quiet under the desk)
